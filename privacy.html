Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

At Nexoura, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the necessary information when you interact with us or make a purchase:

  • Contact Information: Name, Email Address, and Phone/WhatsApp Number to deliver digital products and respond to inquiries.
  • Payment Information: To verify transactions (e.g., Vodafone Cash or InstaPay receipt screenshots/numbers). We do not store or process credit card numbers directly on our servers; they are handled securely by trusted third-party payment gateways.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to:

  • Process your digital product orders and send download links securely.
  • Provide 24/7 customer support and handle any technical queries.
  • Improve our website performance and user experience analytics.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement standard, robust security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We will never rent, sell, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain integrations or links to third-party providers (like payment gateways, form handlers like Formspree, or social platforms like WhatsApp). Please note that these third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we do not accept any responsibility or liability for their policies.

5. Cookies

We use local storage/session storage to manage basic interface experiences, such as your dark/light theme preference, the exit-intent popup session history, and countdown timers. We do not use intrusive tracking cookies.

6. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated "Last Updated" date at the top.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@nexoura.org.
